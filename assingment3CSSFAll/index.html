History

Alpenliebe, a beloved caramel candy brand, is part of the Perfetti Van Melle confectionery group formed in 2001 through the merger of Italian company Perfetti and Dutch company Van Melle. Perfetti, founded in 1946, and Van Melle, established in 1900, brought their confectionery expertise to create a global powerhouse. Alpenliebe, known for its creamy caramel candies, has become a staple in the brand's diverse product portfolio. While specific details about the candy's origins may not be extensively documented, its success is evident through its popularity and the brand's global reach, offering various flavors and formulations to cater to different tastes.
